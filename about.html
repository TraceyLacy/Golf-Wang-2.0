About Golf Wang

Golf Wang is a fashion and streetwear brand created by Tyler, the Creator in 2011. It reflects his creativity and unique personality through clothing.

Style

The brand is known for its bright colours, bold patterns, and unusual designs. It stands out from typical streetwear by embracing individuality and self-expression.

Origin of the Name

The name "Golf Wang" is a remix of "Wolf Gang," which comes from Tyler's music group, Odd Future. This reflects the playful and creative nature of the brand.
