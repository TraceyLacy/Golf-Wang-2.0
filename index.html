ABOUT

Golf Wang is a fashion/streetwear brand created by Tyler, the Creator. It started in 2011 and is known for bright colours and bold, unusual designs. Tyler expresses himself through both his music and his clothing brand. The name "Golf Wang" is a remix of "Wolf Gang", inspired by his music group Odd Future.
