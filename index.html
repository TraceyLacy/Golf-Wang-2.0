ABOUT

Golf Wang is a fashion/streetwear brand created by Tyler, the Creator. It started in 2011. The style features bright colours and unusual designs. Tyler expresses himself through his music and clothing. The name "Golf Wang" is a remix of "Wolf Gang," inspired by his music group Odd Future.
